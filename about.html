About Us

You are not alone. Recovery starts with one step.

Siya Drug Rehabilitation Centre is a youth-focused organization dedicated to helping individuals overcome drug and substance abuse. We provide professional care, counseling, and life-skills development to empower young people to rebuild their lives and achieve a healthier future.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To support and empower youth to overcome substance abuse through rehabilitation, education, and community support.

Vision

A drug-free generation where young people live healthy, productive, and fulfilling lives.

Main Message / Goal

  • Promote a drug-free lifestyle among youth
  • Emphasize that recovery is possible
  • Provide support, hope, and guidance
  • Reduce stigma around addiction and rehabilitation
