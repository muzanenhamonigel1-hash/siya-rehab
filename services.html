Services / Programs

Support, recovery, and positive life choices

Siya Drug Rehabilitation Centre provides rehabilitation services and support programs designed to help youth and families. We also focus on prevention and awareness to promote a drug-free lifestyle and reduce stigma around addiction and rehabilitation.

Start Your Recovery Journey Today Reach Out—We’re Here for You

Main Goal

Promote a drug-free lifestyle among youth.

Emphasize that recovery is possible.

Provide support, hope, and guidance.

Reduce stigma around addiction and rehabilitation.

Core message:
“You are not alone. Recovery starts with one step.”
